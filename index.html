RONALDO VOTED MARCH'S PLAYER OF THE MONTH

Cristiano Ronaldo’s hat-trick against Tottenham Hotspur has catapulted him to Manchester United's Player of the Month

Ronaldo fended off competition from Jadon Sancho and Fred, who both had an impressive month themselves, and with three goals in just two appearances, took home the prize.Our no.7 last won the award in October, when he was named Player of the Month for the second consecutive time following his scintillating start to the season after returning to the Premier League in the summer.Ronaldo picked up the player of the month award following a stunning hat trick for United against Spurs in a crucial Premier League game.Ronaldo’s opening goal against Spurs has also been nominated for the Premier League goal of the month award for March.
